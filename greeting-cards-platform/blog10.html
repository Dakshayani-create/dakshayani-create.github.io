DIY Card Ideas: Adding a Personal Touch

While store-bought cards are beautiful and convenient, there is something incredibly special about a card that you've crafted yourself. It shows a level of thought and effort that money just can't buy.

You don't need to be an expert artist to create a beautiful DIY card. With a few simple materials, you can create something truly unique.

Creative Ideas

  • Pressed Flowers: Add dried, pressed flowers to the front of a blank card for a delicate, natural look.
  • Photo Collage: Print out small photos of you and the recipient and arrange them creatively on the card stock.
  • Watercolor Wash: A simple, abstract watercolor wash in their favorite colors makes a beautiful backdrop for calligraphy.

Tips for Success

Invest in good quality, heavy cardstock. Flimsy paper won't hold up to glue or paint well. Always practice your design and your lettering on a scrap piece of paper before committing to the final card.

Remember, the imperfections are part of the charm. A DIY card doesn't need to look factory-made; its beauty lies in the fact that it was made by human hands.
