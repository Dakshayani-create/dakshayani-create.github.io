Funny Birthday Wishes That Will Make Them Laugh Out Loud

Not every birthday card needs to be a tearjerker. If the birthday boy or girl has a great sense of humor, a funny card is often the most memorable one they'll receive.

Poking a little fun at their age, your friendship, or their quirky habits is a great way to celebrate another trip around the sun.

Best Messages to Include

  • "Happy Birthday! I was going to make an age joke, but I remembered how much you complain when your back hurts."
  • "You're not old, you're just... vintage. Happy Birthday!"
  • "I'm so glad we're going to grow old together, and that you have a head start. Happy Birthday!"

Tips to Write Wishes

Know your audience. Make sure the humor matches their personality. What's hilarious to your best friend might not be appropriate for your boss or your grandmother.

Pair a joke with a genuine closing. Even if the card is meant to be funny, ending with a sincere "Hope you have a great day" softens the teasing and leaves them smiling.
